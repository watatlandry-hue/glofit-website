FAQ

Questions, answered

```
How does the AI body projection work?
Upload four guided photos — front, profile, back. Our AI analyzes your body composition and generates a realistic projection of your body once you reach your goal. The projection updates every four weeks based on your actual progress. Your photos are encrypted and never shared.
Which languages does Glofit support?
Glofit automatically detects your language based on your App Store region or device settings. Currently supported: English (USA, Canada, UK, Australia, and others) and Français (Québec, France, Belgique, Suisse). You can change languages anytime in Settings.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All photos are encrypted at rest and in transit. We comply with GDPR (Europe), CCPA (California) and PIPEDA (Canada). We never sell your data, never share your photos, and you can delete your account and all data anytime from Settings.
Do I need a gym membership?
No. When you sign up, you tell Glofit what equipment you have access to — full gym, home setup, just bodyweight, or outdoor-only. Your AI-generated workout plan adapts accordingly. Glofit works whether you train in a commercial gym, your living room, or a local park.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime in the App Store or Google Play subscription settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No fees, no questions asked. Your profile and progress data remain available if you decide to return.
When is Glofit available?
Glofit is launching first in the USA and Canada on iOS and Google Play, followed by France and Europe in Phase 2. Join the waitlist below to be notified the moment we launch — early signups get priority access and launch discounts.
```
